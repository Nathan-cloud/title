Message From Our Founder

KENISHA NATIVIDAD, J.D. PRESIDENT/ESCROW OFFICER

Meet Kenisha KENISHA NATIVIDAD, J.D. PRESIDENT/ESCROW OFFICER Kenisha Natividad is a law school graduate born in San Diego, California. She is a wife, mother of three and a bonus mom of two. She migrated to the Boerne area in 2020

She has shaped her career in human resources and compliance, as well as by helping run a successful logistics company out of West Texas. After receiving her law degree, she began to shift her focus to the title industry. Creating the boutique experience known as Apirra

She opened her branch in Boerne in September of 2021 with the hopes of bringing the focus back to the people. She has since migrated back to her hometown of Odessa Texas as of July 2022 to create an extension of what proves to be what is missing in the title industry. Apirra now has two locations servicing a boutique title experience.

Her ability to shift and adjust to her surroundings, and to resolve any issue or task that comes her way, makes her a powerhouse of getting things done. She holds herself accountable and is dedicated to the mission of Apirra. As she strives to educate others on the title process, she continues to focus on educating herself and staying on top of the industries shifts.
